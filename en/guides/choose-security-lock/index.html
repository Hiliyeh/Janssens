Types of Locks

European Cylinder

The most common, compatible with most doors. Easy to replace.

Multipoint Lock

3, 5 or 7 locking points for maximum security against break-ins.

A2P Lock

Certified by CNPP, resistance tested against break-ins. Top security.

Smart Lock

Opening by smartphone, code or badge. Ideal for short-term rentals.

A2P Certification

The A2P standard (Insurance Prevention Protection) certifies lock resistance:

A2P 1 Star

Resists 5 minutes against break-in attempt

A2P 2 Stars

Resists 10 minutes - Recommended for apartments

A2P 3 Stars

Resists 15 minutes - Maximum protection

An A2P lock is only effective if the door itself is solid.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best lock for a front door?

For a front door, we recommend a multipoint lock certified A2P 2 stars minimum, with a high-security cylinder.

How much does a good lock cost?

Price varies according to security level. Contact us for a free quote tailored to your needs.

Can I install the lock myself?

For a simple lock yes, but for a multipoint or A2P lock, professional installation is recommended to ensure security.
