Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a door opening cost?

The price depends on the type of lock and the complexity of the intervention. We give you a free quote on site, before any intervention. No unpleasant surprises: the announced price is the final price.

Can you open an armored door?

Yes, we work on all types of doors: armored doors, standard doors, apartment building entrance doors, and all lock brands (Fichet, Vachette, Bricard, etc.).

Will the opening damage my door?

In 95% of cases, we open the door without any damage thanks to our non-destructive techniques. If damage is necessary (rare), we warn you beforehand and you can decide.

Do you work at night and on weekends?

Yes, we are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. A surcharge may apply for night interventions.
