Who are we?

Janssens Serrurier is a professional locksmith company based in Brussels. We operate throughout the Brussels-Capital Region, Walloon Brabant and Flemish Brabant for all your locksmith needs: emergencies, installations, repairs and security advice.

Our mission is simple: to provide you with fast, honest and professional locksmith service, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Whether you're locked out at 3am or want to improve your home security, we're here for you.

Our values

Speed

Intervention within 30 minutes on average. 24/7 emergency service, including holidays.

Transparency

Free quote and price given by phone before intervention. No surprises, no hidden fees.

Expertise

Non-destructive opening in 95% of cases. Qualified technicians with over 10 years of experience.
