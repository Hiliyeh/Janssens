Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a lock replacement cost?

The price depends on the type of lock chosen. A standard cylinder costs less than a Fichet high-security cylinder. We provide a detailed quote before any work.

Quelle lock choose for ma door ?

We advise you based on your budget and needs. For optimal security, we recommend A2P certified cylinders (Fichet, Mul-T-Lock, Vachette).

How Long Does Installation Take?

A cylinder replacement takes about 15-30 minutes. A complete multipoint lock can take 1-2 hours depending on the model.

Do You Have Parts in Stock?

We have the most common cylinders and locks in our vehicle. For specific models, we can order quickly.
