Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to open a locked door?

The price dépend du type de lock and de l'heure d'intervention. Nous you donnons a free quote on site before any intervention. Le announced price est the final price, without surprise.

Will the opening damage my lock?

Dans 95% cas, we open the door without aucun dégât. Votre lock reste parfaitement fonctionnelle. Si exceptionnellement a dommage est necessary, we you prévenons avant.

How long to arrive?

Notre délai moyen est de 30 minutes in Brussels and surroundings. Nous you donnons a délai précis au téléphone.

Do you work at night and on weekends?

Yes, we are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. A surcharge may apply for night interventions.
